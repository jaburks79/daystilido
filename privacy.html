Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

DaysTilIDo.com does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your names, wedding date, and ceremony time are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to any server.

Local storage

If you check "Remember our wedding," your details are saved in your browser's local storage on your device only. We have no access to this data.

Advertising

DaysTilIDo.com displays advertisements to support the free service. Ad networks may use cookies to serve relevant ads.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate traffic. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Contact

Questions? Reach out via the feedback link on the main page.
