How to use the wedding countdown

Enter your names, wedding date, and ceremony time, then press Start. The timer counts down in real time — days, hours, minutes, and seconds — until your ceremony begins. Share the page with your partner, bridesmaids, groomsmen, and family so everyone can count down together.

Will it remember our wedding date?

Check "Remember our wedding" and your details will be saved on your device. Next time you visit, your countdown picks up right where it left off — no re-entering needed.

Can I share this with my wedding party?

Absolutely — just share the link to daystilido.com with your bridesmaids, groomsmen, and family. Have everyone enter the same date and you can all count down together from anywhere in the world.

How far in advance can I set it?

As far out as you'd like — just got engaged and your wedding is two years away? No problem. The countdown will tick down from years all the way to the final seconds before you walk down the aisle.

Does it count down to the exact ceremony time?

Yes — enter your ceremony time and the countdown will tick down to the exact minute your wedding begins, not just the date.

Who is this for?

Anyone planning a wedding — engaged couples, brides, grooms, bridesmaids, and excited family members. Whether your wedding is next week or next year, Days Til I Do helps you savor every moment of the countdown.
